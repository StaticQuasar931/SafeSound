About & Licenses

SafeSound is built for school-safe listening. It does not connect to Spotify, YouTube, Apple Music, or any commercial streaming service. It does not support uploads, chat, comments, downloads from third-party services, or user-generated audio sharing.

This starter template includes original demo tracks generated by the app for safe testing. Replace them only with tracks that are clearly public-domain, CC0, or otherwise licensed for redistribution in your project.
