How this stays legal

Custom tracks are generated in your browser from note patterns you choose. That means you are not copying music from outside services, and your created tracks stay local to this browser.

If you write lyrics, keep them original and school-safe. SafeSound will only display the lyrics you type yourself.

Legal transparency

About & Licenses

SafeSound does not connect to Spotify, YouTube, Apple Music, or any commercial streaming service. It does not support uploads, comments, chat, or third-party downloading.

The starter library contains original demo music bundled for safe testing and locally created custom songs stored only in this browser. Replace demo entries only with music that is clearly public-domain, CC0, or otherwise redistributable.
